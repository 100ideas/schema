A part of a successively published publication such as a periodical or multi-volume work, often numbered. It may represent a time span, such as a year. Often embedded as the isPartOf property in a Publication Issue.

Examples

An example of a volume of the journal Nature is below. The volume has a volumeNumber and isPartOf the Periodical with the title "Nature". 

{
  "type": "PublicationVolume",
  "volumeNumber": 571,
  "isPartOf": {
    "type": "Periodical",
    "title": "Nature"
  }
}
