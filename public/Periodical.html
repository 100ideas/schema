A publication in any medium issued in successive parts bearing numerical or chronological designations and intended, such as a magazine, scholarly journal, or newspaper to continue indefinitely. Often embedded as the isPartOf property in a Publication Volume.

Examples

An example of the journal Nature is below. The dateStart is the date it was first published. The first issn numbers is for the printed journal and the second for the online edition.

{
  "type": "Periodical",
  "title": "Nature",
  "issn": ["0028-0836", "1476-4687"],
  "dateStart": "1869-11-04",
  "url": "https://www.nature.com/"
}
